--- n: 3 name: "State: Berserking" param1: "Irrelevant" param2: "Type" bg1: 1 bg2: 1 bgee: 1 iwd1: 0 iwd2: 0 pst: 1 --- Applies the berserk effect to the targeted creature(s) in the style specified by the 'Type' parameter.
Known values for 'Type' are:
0 Default/In Combat -> Only berserk while in combat.
1 Constant -> always berserk, sets SPELLSTATE 39 (only in EEs).
2 Blood Rage -> always berserk, sets SPELLSTATE 35 (only in EEs)
Target will uncontrollably attack enemies if any are in view, and will occasionally attack neutral creatures. In Combat(0) = Only berserk while in combat. Constant(1) = always berserk, sets SPELLSTATE 39. Usable against party members and non-party members. Forces creature to use a melee weapon if possible.

The affected creature becomes berserk and its AI scripts stop running. Grants a +2 bonus to hit and damage with all weapons. It also sets the STATE_BERSERK flag.
