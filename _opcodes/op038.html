--- n: 38 opname: "State: Silence" param1: "Irrelevant" param2: "Irrelevant" bg1: 1 bg2: 1 bgee: 1 iwd1: 1 iwd2: 1 pst: 1 pstee: 1 --- Applies the silence effect to the targeted creature(s). Sets STATE_SILENCED to true.
A silenced creature cannot cast spells / use special abilities (unless flagged as Castable when silenced) or initiate dialog.
The engine is hard-coded to allow these spells to be cast by a silenced creature:
  • "SPWI219" (Vocalize)
  • "SPIN649" (Teleport to Pocket Plane) in BG2/BGEE
