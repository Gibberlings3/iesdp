--- n: 123 name: "Item: Remove Inventory Item" param1: "Irrelevant" param2: "Irrelevant" bg1: 1 bg2: 1 bgee: 1 iwd1: 1 iwd2: 1 pst: 1 --- Removes the item specified by the resource key from the targeted creature(s). This effect should be used to remove inventory items (use opcode #112 for equipped items).
  • Removes all item(s) in the first slot containing the specified resource key from the targeted creature(s).
  • Items in container items (e.g. Bags of Holding) will not be removed.
  • Inventory slots are checked in numerical order from SLOTS.IDS.
