--- n: 145 name: "Spell: Disable Spell Casting Abilities" param1: "Irrelevant" param2: "Spell Type" special: "Show message?" bg1: 1 bg2: 1 bgee: 1 iwd1: 1 iwd2: 0 pst: 1 --- Disables spell casting abilities of the targeted creature(s), in the style specified by the Spell Type field.
Known values for Spell Type are:
  • 0 ⟶ Wizard
  • 1 ⟶ Priest
  • 2 ⟶ Innate
  • 3 ⟶ Innate, only 'magical' abilities (SPL flag Ignore dead/wild magic unset) (EEs)

Known values for Show message? are:
  • 0 ⟶ Yes
  • 1 ⟶ No
