The EFF can be removed via the Remove Curse opcode (in addition to other standard methods).
Known values for IDS File are:
  • 2 ⟶ EA.IDS
  • 3 ⟶ GENERAL.IDS
  • 4 ⟶ RACE.IDS
  • 5 ⟶ CLASS.IDS
  • 6 ⟶ SPECIFIC.IDS
  • 7 ⟶ GENDER.IDS
  • 8 ⟶ ALIGN.IDS
  • 9 ⟶ KIT.IDS
