This file defines the spell gaining tables per level.
Where:
First colunm is the PC's/NPC's level.
Columns 2 and up consist of number of spells that CRE should have on the specific level in their spell book.
Note that the BG2 manual is incorrect in regards to the casting level for rangers and paladins. Rangers cast at a spell level 7 levels below their current level, while paladins cast at a spell level 8 below their current level. This casting level is not capped at 9.
