BG2: ToB Identifiers

The first character of the numeric code identifies the spell prefix:
1 SPPR
2 SPWI
3 SPIN
4 SPCL
Anything else defaults to MARW.

The remaining three characters identify the spell filename, i.e. 1101 refers to SPPR101.
