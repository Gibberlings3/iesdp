--- title: "PSTEE Identifiers : class.ids" ---
PSTEE Identifiers

Relates to offset 0x273 in the CRE file format.
For use with the ChangeClass() action.
For use with the Class() and OriginalClass() triggers.

IDS V1.0

1 MAGE
Detects mages (and sorcerers), though only single class & kits.

2 FIGHTER
Detects fighters (and monks), though only single class & kits.

3 CLERIC
Detects clerics, though only single class & kits.

4 THIEF
Detects thieves, though only single class & kits.

5 BARD
Detects bards, though only single class & kits.

6 PALADIN
Detects paladins, though only single class & kits.

7 FIGHTER_MAGE
8 FIGHTER_CLERIC
9 FIGHTER_THIEF
10 FIGHTER_MAGE_THIEF

11 DRUID
Detects druids, though only single class & kits.

12 RANGER
Detects ranger, though only single class & kits.

13 MAGE_THIEF
14 CLERIC_MAGE
15 CLERIC_THIEF
16 FIGHTER_DRUID
17 FIGHTER_MAGE_CLERIC
18 CLERIC_RANGER

19 SORCERER
Detects sorcerers, though only single class.

20 MONK
Detects monks, though only single class.

202 MAGE_ALL
Detects mages, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class, as well as sorcerers .
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

203 FIGHTER_ALL
Detects fighters, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class, as well as monks.
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

204 CLERIC_ALL
Detects clerics, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class.
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

205 THIEF_ALL
Detects thieves, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class.
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

206 BARD_ALL
Detects bards, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class.
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

207 PALADIN_ALL
Detects paladins, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class.
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

208 DRUID_ALL
Detects druids, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class.
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

209 RANGER_ALL
Detects rangers, including single class, kits, multi-class, and dual-class.
Dual-classed characters will detect only as their new class until their original class is re-activated, then they will detect as a multi-classed character.

255 NO_CLASS
